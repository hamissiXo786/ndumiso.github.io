State of the Art App

This is a 4IR application which reduces congestion at the port by taking cognisance of the time period the gantry crane takes to off load and pace a container on a truck, that cycle time interval is then displayed to all the trucks scheduled for entry into the port so that they know as to whether to arrive early or late because if there is a delay the cycle time changes and is made transparent to all the trucks so that they act accordingly.

Icon
Truck Monitoring

The app is connected to google maps so you are able to get the precise geographical location of the truck, the alternative routes possible as well as if there is traffic or not.

Icon
Mitigation of Fuel Theft

The app comes with a built in feature that is connected to sensors in the engine compartment with senses which monitor fuel usage, the speed of the truck as well as the overall peformance of the truck.

The app also comes with a pdf reader and storage equipeed with the latest anti-virus and only accessible from the activation of the truck driver, the trucking company as well as the clearing agent. It stores the relevant cross-boarder documentation.

Truck to Truck Communication

The app is able to communicate with similar devices on other trucks which ensures driver focus on the route because a notification pops up on the device and the driver acknowledges it without removing their hands of the steering wheel.
